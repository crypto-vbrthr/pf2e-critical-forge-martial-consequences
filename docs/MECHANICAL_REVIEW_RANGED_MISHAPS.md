## Review Scope

The review examined schema validity, startup behavior, rules clarity, mechanical uniqueness, overlap with Critical Forge Core, Martial Attack Fumbles, and Arsenal, relative power, timing, action economy, ranged-weapon compatibility, and table usability.

## Complete Redesigns

The following cards were redesigned:

- RM-002 Revealing Shot
- RM-004 Broken Firing Rhythm
- RM-005 Forced Reposition
- RM-006 Target Finds Cover
- RM-007 Poor Follow-Through
- RM-008 Obstructed Arc
- RM-014 Follow the Shot
- RM-017 A Very Encouraging Miss
- RM-019 Helpful Warning

The revisions replaced redundant movement choices, duplicate free Steps, narrow reaction restrictions, invalid Point Out and Aid usage, and several effects that could expire without producing a meaningful result.

## Targeted Adjustments

- RM-009 is now rated Light because it depends on the source already having cover.
- RM-010 is now rated Moderate and has normal draw weight.
- RM-015 restricts its reactive Step to spaces where the target remains a valid target.
- RM-020 now supports an eligible ally and includes reliable ranged-attack or movement fallbacks.
- RM-025, RM-028, and RM-030 no longer restate the normal reaction behavior of Step.

## Result

**Mechanical Review: Passed after revision**

The pack now has a clearer identity centered on firing positions, cover, trajectory, predictable lanes, counterfire, environmental complications, and target-side tactical responses rather than generic martial penalties.
