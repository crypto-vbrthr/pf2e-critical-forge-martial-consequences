## Review Scope

The review examined schema validity, startup behavior, rules clarity, mechanical uniqueness, overlap with Critical Forge Core and Arsenal, relative power, timing, action economy, creature compatibility, and table usability.

## Complete Redesigns

The following cards were redesigned:

- MAF-001 Lost Footing
- MAF-004 Slippery Grip
- MAF-006 Wild Follow-Through
- MAF-008 Distracted Recovery
- MAF-009 Defensive Collapse
- MAF-010 Unsteady Recovery
- MAF-027 False Opening
- MAF-028 Exposed Flank
- MAF-030 Guarded the Wrong Side

The revisions replaced repeated Off-Guard, Dazzled, AC, attack-roll, and Speed penalties with consequences based on movement cost, action sequencing, immediate Feinting, moving through occupied space, delayed Steps, claimed squares, and flanking support.

## Targeted Adjustments

- MAF-013 now requires a generic recovery action rather than Interact, preserving compatibility with unarmed and natural attacks.
- MAF-021 is consumed only by a Strike that is subject to a multiple attack penalty.
- MAF-022 now has weight 1 and clarifies common Reflex DC interactions.
- MAF-026 is rated Light because its trigger is conditional.
- MAF-029 includes a Step reaction fallback when the target has no eligible existing reaction.

## Result

**Mechanical Review: Passed after revision**
